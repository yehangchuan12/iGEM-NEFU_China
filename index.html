Based on research findings, it is projected that the global population of the individuals diagnosed as myopia will reach a staggering number of 5 billion by the year 2050.

Myopia has become a prominent factor leading to permanent blindness worldwide[1]. The occurrence of myopia is difficult to prevent, and it is often associated with prolonged periods of studying and improper eye usage.

The optimization of metabolic engineering processes can significantly enhance the efficiency of biological production. By gene overexpressing and employing CRISPRi technology for multi-level gene interference, a high-yield substrate E. coli chassis strain was constructed.

How to fight against myopia? Glasses-- May compress the nasal bridge, especially harmful for teenagers who are experiencing physical growth. Contact lenses-- May result in eye infection. Operation-- May cause sequela like dry eye.

The efficacy of adenosine receptor (ADOR) antagonists in mitigating the progression of myopia has been demonstrated in both human subjects and animal models [2].

Among them, intake of 7-methylxanthine (7-MX) is associated with efficacy of alleviating school myopia in children[3], which is proven as a non-toxic and effective compound to reduce myopia progression and axial eye growth in both pre-clinical and clinical studies[4]

7-MX is one of the ideal compounds for orally administered treatments, which usually have considerably preventive effects on progressive myopia without side effects [5,6].

Lofty price is a significant factor limiting the entry of 7-MX into the public's view. Another aspect to consider is the appropriate form to adopt in order to make 7-MX enter the market and get consumers’ favor.

The biomanufacturing process to convert caffeine into 7-MX has a great potential to decrease the cost of 7-MX production. Combining 7-MX with coffee, an internationally popular beverage in the modern society, has the potential to provide individuals, particularly teenagers, with the opportunity to prevent their myopia development when enjoying coffee’s delicacy.
